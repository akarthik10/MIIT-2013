Theme of the Workshop

The main theme of the workshop is to impart knowledge in the area of Machine Intelligence, which combines a wide variety of advanced technologies. It gives machines the ability to learn, adapt, make decisions, and display behaviors not explicitly programmed into their original capabilities. Machine Intelligence capabilities add powerful, analytical, self-tuning and adaptive behavior to applications.

This workshop provides a platform for enhancing and exchanging knowledge of experts from academia, R&D, and Industry in the area of Machine Intelligence and Imaging Technologies.

Scope of the Workshop

This workshop covers the following topics

  • Computer Vision
  • Pattern Analysis and Recognition
  • Machine learning
  • Medical Image Analysis
  • Content-based Image retrieval
  • Recent trends in Data mining and Analysis

Hands on MATLAB by HCL Technologies and Data mining/Machine Intelligence by IBM.

About BIT

Bangalore Institute of Technology

Bangalore Institute of Technology came into existence in August 1979, under the auspices of Vokkaligara Sangha, Bangalore. The institute is provided with the necessary modern infrastructural facilities. BIT has highly qualified and experienced teachers. The college is affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University and accredited by National Board of Accreditation, New Delhi. The institute offers 9 undergraduate, 6 post graduate and research centers for carrying out Ph.D degree with an overall student strength of over 4000. The institute has tie up with other organizations like IISc, NAL, ISRO for carrying out inter disciplinary research and collaborative programmes.

Department of Computer Science and Engineering

The Computer Science & Engineering department was started in the year 1981 with the mission to impart high quality education. The department is accredited by NBA, New Delhi for the second time and has been granted with permanent affiliation by VTU. The department has an infrastructure of six computer labs equipped with latest computers and software under high speed network having internet connectivity, high-tech class rooms, library, an exclusive project lab, etc. The department also runs M. Tech (Computer Science & Engineering) and Doctoral degree programs. More than 100 research papers have been published by the department faculties in the international journals and conference proceedings. These papers were presented in USA, China, Singapore, and Thailand. The student projects have obtained recognition by the leading Software Industries, IISc and other institutions. The department is a member of CSI, India.
